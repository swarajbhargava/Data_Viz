Welcome to the G7 Economic Observatory

Welcome to our website dedicated to visualizing macroeconomic data for the G7 countries. This website is designed to provide users with an interactive and insightful view of the economic performance and growth of the world's largest advanced economies.
The G7 countries, which include the United States, Canada, Japan, Germany, France, Italy, and the United Kingdom, account for nearly 50% of the global economy. As such, understanding the economic trends and dynamics of these nations is critical for policymakers, investors, and businesses alike.

Our website provides users with a comprehensive look at the macroeconomic data for each of the G7 nations, including key indicators such as GDP, inflation, employment, and trade. We present this data in a visually compelling way through a variety of charts, graphs, and interactive data visualizations.
